# Design Revision Notes
Following the heuristic evaluation, two major usability issues were prioritised  and addressed in the Figma prototype.

## Revision 1: Mood Tracker Chart

### Problem
The mood tracker chart did not include axis labels or a legend. This made it  difficult for users to interpret the weekly mood trend.

### Fix Applied
A labelled x-axis was added to show the days of the week. A y-axis was added to  show the mood scale from 1 to 5. A short legend was also included to clarify  the meaning of the chart.

### Reason
This improves clarity and supports the heuristic of matching the system with  the real world.

## Revision 2: Booking Form Optional Field

### Problem
The “Reason for Visit” field on the booking page did not clearly indicate  whether it was required or optional.

### Fix Applied
The label was updated to include “Optional”, and a short reassurance note was  added below the field.

### Reason
This reduces user hesitation and supports error prevention by making form  expectations clear.
